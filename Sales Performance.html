Sales Performance Report

A data-driven Power BI project designed to analyze overall sales performance across customers, products, and sales teams. The dashboard provides insights into total revenue, profit, and units sold, highlights top-performing salespersons and regions, and identifies key growth opportunities through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

Storytelling with KPIs

1. Executive Summary

This dashboard provides an overall snapshot of business performance. The company generated a total revenue of 23M, incurred total costs of 17M, and achieved a gross profit of 6M with 123K units sold. The bar chart shows top-performing companies like Horizon Foods and Stellar Retail, which contribute majorly to total revenue. The industry analysis highlights that Retail and Engineering sectors are key contributors.Monthly trends indicate consistent growth across industries over time.

Key Insight: Strong revenue generation across multiple industries with Retail leading the chart.

Consistent upward trend shows stable market performance.

2. Product Analysis

This slide breaks down revenue by product category and individual products. Core Processing leads as the highest-revenue category, followed by Output Devices and System & Power. The product-level analysis shows that Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) and Central Processing Unit (CPU) are top sellers with over 4M+ revenue each. Monthly trend lines indicate steady performance, with slight peaks in March and July.

Key Insight: High-margin technical products like GPUs and CPUs are major growth drivers, indicating strong demand for high-performance computing components.

3. Customer Analysis

Our overall business performance shows a Total Revenue of 23M, Total Cost of 17M, resulting in a Gross Profit of 6M, with 123K units sold. The Retail industry is the top revenue contributor, followed by Engineering and Finance sectors. Among customers, Horizon Foods (4.2M), Stellar Retail (3.5M), and Quantum Dynamics (3.3M) are the top three revenue-generating clients. The trend analysis shows steady revenue growth across industries, with a consistent upward pattern from March to August 2025.

Key Insight: The business has a diverse and balanced customer base, but Retail and Engineering clients dominate overall sales.

Strengthening relationships with top customers and replicating success in underperforming sectors can drive further growth.

The sales analytics project provided actionable insights into revenue patterns, product demand, and regional growth.
